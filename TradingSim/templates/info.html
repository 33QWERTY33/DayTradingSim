Info

Overview

This website will allow you to create an account, and trade stocks and ETF's using fake money, but with real time data.

In the future There will be an API endpoint to allow you to remotely and automatically perform operations with the site.

The purpose of this site is to help people refine their day trading skills with absolutely zero risk

Usage guide

You will need to create an account before you can access the full functionality of the page

You can find the sign up button underneath the Account Management dropdown in the navbar

Each new account will be given 25,000 dollars to trade with as that complies with the PDT rule

You can use quick search or Ticker Search to look up current information about a stock that was scraped from Yahoo Finance

In the home page when you are logged in you will see two buttons, buy order and sell order

The Buy Order button will take you to the page needed for you to fill out a buy order form

The Sell Order button will take you to the page needed for you to fill out a sell order form, after completing this action your profit will be calculated

The Buy Order form has 3 inputs, ticker, stock amount and sell trigger

ticker is the stock ticker of the stock you want to buy

stock amount is the amount of stocks you want to purchase (the amount of money you spend will be the stock amount times the current price)

the sell trigger is the amount you want to sell the stock at, this will be done automatically for you when the stock reaches that price
